Frequently Asked Questions about Moe

Q. How tall are you?

A: A proud 5 feet! I bring all the personality of someone taller… with a little less reach.

Q: What's your hidden talent?

A: I can talk to computers… they don’t always listen, but we’re working on our communication skills.

Q. What's your idea of a fun time?

A: Building apps, especially C++ bartending apps—who knew code and cocktails could mix so well?

Q: Do you have a vehicle?

A: Oh, you mean my trusty car, Mater? Absolutely! He’s mostly reliable and has proven his worth from the skyscrapers of New York to the sunshine of Florida.

Q: What's your experience with technology?

A: I've got Windows, macOS, and a bit of Azure under my belt, and my skills include taming laptops, wrangling Active Directory, and troubleshooting remotely. If it has buttons, I can probably fix it (or at least Google how to).

Q: What's your biggest strength in a team?

A: I bring a solid mix of patience, tech know-how, and humor (for when things inevitably don’t go as planned). Also, I’m not afraid of tackling the infamous “Have you tried turning it off and on again?” question.

Q: Are you a detail-oriented person?

A: Let’s just say I measured the width of my own pinky nail once, just to make sure it’s accurate. So yes, details are definitely my thing.

Q: How do you stay current with your skills?

A: I’m constantly learning, whether it’s upgrading my C++ skills for a bartending app or studying for certifications. And if I need advice, the internet is full of “mentors”—as long as they’re under the character limit.

I’m currently jobless and trying my hand at comedy.

So technically, I’m either on the brink of fame or just really, really unemployed!
